Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Viral Reels Generator collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted)
  • API Keys: Facebook Page Access Tokens, AI provider API keys (stored securely)
  • Content: Scripts you create, videos you generate
  • Usage Data: Video generation history, posting schedules

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate and post videos to your Facebook Page
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about updates and security

3. Data Storage & Security

Storage: All data is stored locally on your server in encrypted SQLite databases.

Security: We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Password encryption using bcrypt
  • Secure session management
  • Protected API key storage
  • HTTPS encryption (when deployed)

4. Facebook Integration

When you connect your Facebook Page:

  • We use Facebook OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • We request permissions to post videos to your Page
  • We store your Page Access Token securely
  • We never post to your personal profile, only your Page

Important: We never sell your data to third parties. Your Facebook tokens are used solely to post videos to your own Page.

5. AI Provider Integrations

If you connect AI providers (Claude, OpenRouter, GLM):

  • Your API keys are stored securely in our database
  • We only use these keys to generate scripts as you request
  • We never share your API keys with third parties
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Settings page

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Generated Content: Stored until you delete it
  • API Keys: Stored until you remove them from Settings
  • Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data in the Dashboard
  • Delete: Remove videos, scripts, or your entire account
  • Export: Download your data (contact us for assistance)
  • Revoke: Disconnect Facebook or AI providers anytime

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Facebook: For posting videos to your Page
  • AI Providers: For script generation (optional)
  • FFmpeg: For video processing (runs locally)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Viral Reels Generator is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email (for significant changes)

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@viralreels.com
